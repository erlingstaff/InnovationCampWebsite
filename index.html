PROSJEKT "HER"

PROSJEKT "HER" has its purpose to collect data
and facilitate important information regarding absence and dropouts in school.

Our goal is to use the collected data to find out
the key reasons for why some children is not attending school.

Where?: In Redd Barna's report "Save our education"
12 countries are listed as in danger of falling behind the SDG4-goal.
One of these is Nigeria.

Why?: 10,5 million (38%) children does not attend school at all in Nigeria.
"The writing and reading skills here are among the very lowest.
If you are a girl in northeastern Nigeria, you stand at the back in the global schoolclass."

Who?: Children aged 6-11 years. Teachers at the schools is responsible
for follow-up and guidance when using the tablet. But its ultimately students this project aims for.

How?: Implementing the tablets in the schools.
The product and technology should not be used outsite the area of the schools.
This means that both the students and the teachers will need training in how to use the tablet.
